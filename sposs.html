Overview

SPoSS is designed to provide small businesses a simple and straightforward way to digitize their Point of Sales. The system is a single-page web app for two (2) different users; for admins to manage products within the system and for customers to create orders.

  • Provide customers a user-friendly experience when ordering products.

  • Deployment: Deploy application to production environment.
